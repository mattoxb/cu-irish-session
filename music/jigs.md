# Jigs

Jigs are lively tunes in 6/8 time with a distinctive lilting rhythm. The most common types are:

- **Double Jigs**: The standard jig with eighth note patterns
- **Single Jigs**: Feature more quarter notes, giving a hopping feel
- **Slip Jigs**: In 9/8 time with a graceful, sliding quality
